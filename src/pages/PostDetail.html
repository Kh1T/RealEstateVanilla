J Tower 3 Condominium

J Tower 3 is an exceptional residential property brought to you by a renowned developer with a proven track record of success. With three previous projects successfully completed.

Situated in Tonle Bassac, Khan Chomkarmon, in the vibrant city of Phnom Penh, this property combines luxurious amenities with a prime location to offer an unrivaled living experience.

J Tower 3 stands tall with its magnificent architecture, consisting of 2 underground floors and 77 floors above ground, reaching a soaring height of 310.5m. The building encompasses a total area of 98480 m2, constructed with a sturdy reinforced concrete structure, ensuring durability and safety for its residents.

The developer's attention to detail is evident in the 360 units' design at J Tower 3. Each unit is thoughtfully crafted as a 3-bedroom residence, offering two exclusive area options of 166m2 and 178m2. The developer's commitment to quality and luxury is reflected in every aspect of these elegant and modern living spaces.

Parking is made convenient with 252 rental parking lots available within the premises. Additionally, residents can enjoy smooth and efficient vertical transportation facilitated by the eight elevators installed throughout the building.

J Tower 3 presents a wealth of luxurious facilities spread across various floors. From the main floor on the 50th floor, with its cafe and lounge, wine cooler, mini shop, and business room, to the sky pool and sky bar on the 77th floor, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding cityscape, every amenity has been carefully designed to exceed residents' expectations. The 49th floor boasts an inner pool, kids pool, sauna, public bath, and spa, ensuring residents can relax and rejuvenate.

The 29th floor features a swimming pool and fitness gym, catering to the residents' active lifestyle. For families, the 11th floor offers a dedicated Kids' Play Space, Pool Bar (Billiard Hall), and Table Tennis Hall.

Nature enthusiasts can enjoy the outdoor park on the 28th floor, providing a serene environment amidst the bustling city. For panoramic views of the city skyline, the 78th floor features an observation deck where residents can take in the beauty of Phnom Penh. Other convenient facilities include a laundry service on the 28th floor and a garbage station on every floor.
